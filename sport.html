Actions SportID Name RulesManual Description
1 Football Link A team sport played on a rectangular field with goalposts at each end. Teams score by advancing the ball into the opposing team's end zone for a touchdown or kicking it through the opponent's goalposts for a field goal.
2 Soccer Link Known as football outside North America, it is a team sport played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. Teams score by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal using any part of the body except the hands and arms.
3 Basketball Link A team sport played on a rectangular court where the objective is to score by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. Each team tries to score while preventing the opposing team from scoring.
4 Baseball Link A bat-and-ball game played between two teams who take turns batting and fielding. The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and running to a series of four bases arranged in a diamond shape.
5 Hockey Link A fast-paced team sport played on ice, where the objective is to score by shooting a puck into the opponent's net. Players use sticks to control, pass, and shoot the puck.
